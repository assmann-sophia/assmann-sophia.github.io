Recommended Books about Orcas:

  • "Orca: The Whale Called Killer" by Erich Hoyt - A comprehensive look at the life and behavior of orcas, including their social structures and hunting techniques.
  • "Killer Whales: The Natural History and Genealogy of Orcinus Orca in British Columbia and Washington State" by John K.B. Ford, Graeme M. Ellis, and Kenneth C. Balcomb III - This book provides an in-depth study of the orca populations in the Pacific Northwest.
  • "The Whale Warriors" by Peter Heller - A thrilling account of the efforts to protect orcas and other whales from hunting and environmental threats.
  • "Orcas: The Ocean's Greatest Predators" by Rob Stewart - A visually stunning book that explores the world of orcas through photography and engaging narratives.
  • "Listening to Whales: What the Orcas Have Taught Us" by Alexandra Morton - This book delves into the communication and intelligence of orcas, based on years of research and observation.
